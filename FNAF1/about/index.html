Roblox About Us
Fun Roblox Games

Fun Roblox Games is a website created to make life easier for those who enjoy playing Roblox, Minecraft, and/or any other games that get added to this website in the future. This website was first created for a shortcut to the most popular ROBLOX games, and then became a website for all users to have a shortcut to their favorite game(s).
Frequently Asked Questions
Can I suggest games to add?
Yes, just head over to the live chat and link us to the game. If we are offline, go ahead and leave us an email with a link to the game. We will respond to it as soon as possible.
Are you ROBLOX?
No, we are not ROBLOX, this website is just a shortcut to ROBLOX games.
